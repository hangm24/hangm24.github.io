Timing

Teaching

Learning

Assessment

4 minutes
Starter

Deliever the starter, provide hints to students but not giving out answer directly (praise SEN students to ensure their confidence to proceed)

Students completing the starter questions. Student may seek help if they struggle

Circulate the classroom and check students' answer, to decide if additional modelling is required

4 minutes
Starter Feedback

Go through the answer of the starter, provide modelling if students struggle

Students marking their answer with green pen. Responding to teachers' question when being cold called

Check students' response, award students with positives to improve engagement

8 minutes
Modelling

Explain about the concepts

Listen to teacher and copy down concept bubbles

Check students' response, award students with positives to improve engagement

4 minutes
Mini Whiteboard Question

Put the questions on the board. If students struggle, provide more detailed explanation. Post following up questions if necessary

Work out the mini-whiteboard question and write the answer on the board

Check students' response, award students with positives to improve engagement. Re-assessment if students struggle and time allowed

16 minutes
Independent Task

Provide Hints to students who struggle with the questions.

Live-Mark students' answer and give out positive points base on their progression

Complete the worksheet

Check students' response, award students with positives to improve engagement

4 minutes
Checking Answers

Provide hints to students who struggle with the questions.

Live-Mark students' answer and give out positive points base on their progression

Mark their answer with green pen

Circulate the classroom to check students are checking the answer

Go through challenging questions

5 minutes

Explain on challenging questions if necessary
